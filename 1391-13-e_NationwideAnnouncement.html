Dear OHIO employee,

As an OHIO employee, you are eligible for a discount on auto insurance with Nationwide Insurance®. At the Ohio University Alumni Association, we are proud to partner with organizations, like Nationwide Insurance, that can offer benefits to our alumni as well as Ohio University’s faculty and staff. Nationwide is also offering a Vanishing Deductible benefit that allows you to take $100 off your deductible for very year of safe driving, for up to $500 in savings.

In addition to the faculty and staff benefits, these partnerships also support OUAA programs when faculty and staff purchase products offered by our partner organizations.

Get a quote or call your local agent to see how much you could save.

Sincerely,

The Ohio University Alumni Association
